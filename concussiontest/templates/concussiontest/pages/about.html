A Better Way

The King Devick Concussion Test is an easy way to conduct a sideline assessment for a potential concussion. It begins by checking for NFL guideline “No go” symptoms, any of which result in recommendation for disqualification. If the player passes this initial "No go" assessment, a visual test such as the King-Devick test is used. Each player will establish a baseline time to complete the test. A deviation of more than 3 seconds is a good indication that the player is concussed and should be removed from play.
